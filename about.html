About

Hi! I'm Mrs. Russo. I teach Engineering Graphics at MHS, where I help students explore the fundamentals of 2D and 3D design. The course focuses on both virtual and physical modeling, with hands-on projects like bridge building and 3D printing.


Onshape Preview

Onshape is a cloud-based design software that allows you to create 3D models and drawings from scratch. It’s like using digital tools to build objects, but all your work is saved online, so you can access it from anywhere. It’s used by engineers and designers to create everything from simple objects to complex machines. Since it’s cloud-based, you can collaborate with others and access your projects from any device, making teamwork easy.
